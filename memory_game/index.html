Instructions

Concentration, also knowon as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso, or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

Brain Exercise

It's a well known fact that stimulating your brain in ways that you wouldn't otherwise are great ways to be more mentally aware and promote your overall health. Concentration is one of those ways that can help you stimulate your brain!
